KOKO & FRIENDS

A Post Hardcore Band from Philippines

Koko and Friends is a Post Hardcore band from the Philippines. The band formed in the year 2009, from the city of Tayabas, a well known city in Quezon Province.


Our Members: Sherwin Ablaña Dapito on Vocals, Kenny Denver Sedenio on guitar 1, Ryan Abellanosa on guitar 2, Jan Joseph Yerro on Bass, and Dinno Boy Collantes on drums.

Loud Music, Strong Neck About Koko & Friends

Koko and Friends is a Post Hardcore band from the Philippines. The band formed in the year 2009, from the city of Tayabas, a well known city in Quezon Province. The reason why Koko and friends is the name of our band is because of Francis Jabola Abrigo (a.k.a Koko). Koko is our former bassist. Koko leave the band last year(2010) because of personal issues, like his career. Koko love of music never end there. He is just probably busy about his work and we understand him. We continue performing even without Koko. We replace Koko by Jan Joseph Yerro.
